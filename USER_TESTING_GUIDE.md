## ğŸ¢ Departments

The system has **7 main departments**:

1. **Global** - System administrators with full access
2. **Metrics** - Vice Presidents with cross-department access and metrics viewing
3. **Validation** - Handles job posting validation and mailing team operations
4. **Research** - Handles job posting research and candidate sourcing
5. **Business** - Manages business development and company relationships
6. **Recruitment** - Handles candidate recruitment and assignments
7. **Finance** - Manages financial metrics and reporting

---

## ğŸ“Š Role Hierarchy Structure

The role hierarchy follows a 6-level structure (0-5), where **lower hierarchy numbers indicate higher authority**:

### Hierarchy Level 0: Admin
- **Role**: Admin
- **Description**: Full system access with all permissions
- **Parent**: None (Top level)
- **Department**: Global

### Hierarchy Level 1: VP (Vice President)
- **Role**: VP
- **Description**: Cross-department access, can view all department metrics
- **Parent**: Admin
- **Department**: Metrics

### Hierarchy Level 2: Directors
- **RA Director** (Department: Research) - Parent: VP
- **BDM Director** (Department: Business) - Parent: VP
- **Recruitment Director** (Department: Recruitment) - Parent: VP
- **Finance Director** (Department: Finance) - Parent: VP

### Hierarchy Level 3: Managers
- **RA Manager** (Department: Research) - Parent: RA Director
- **Sr BDM Director** (Department: Business) - Parent: BDM Director
- **Recruitment Manager** (Department: Recruitment) - Parent: Recruitment Director
- **Finance Manager** (Department: Finance) - Parent: Finance Director

### Hierarchy Level 4: Leads
- **RA Lead** (Department: Research) - Parent: RA Manager
- **BDM** (Department: Business) - Parent: Sr BDM Director
- **Recruitment Lead** (Department: Recruitment) - Parent: Recruitment Manager
- **Finance Lead** (Department: Finance) - Parent: Finance Manager

### Hierarchy Level 5: Individual Contributors
- **RA** (Department: Research) - Parent: RA Lead
- **Recruiter** (Department: Recruitment) - Parent: Recruitment Lead
- **Finance Analyst** (Department: Finance) - Parent: Finance Lead

## ğŸ”‘ Key Permissions by Role

### Global Department
- **Admin**: Full access to all resources (create, read, update, delete, manage)
- All permissions across job postings, candidates, companies, users, roles, assignments, and metrics

### Metrics Department
- **VP**: View all metrics across departments
- Read access to job postings, candidates, companies, and assignments

### Validation Department
- **Mailing Team**: Handles job posting validation and mailing operations
- Manages validation workflows for job postings

### Research Department Roles
- **RA Director**: Full management of job postings, candidates, assignments, and department metrics
- **RA Manager**: Create, read, update job postings and candidates; manage assignments
- **RA Lead**: Create, read, update job postings and candidates; create and read assignments
- **RA**: Read-only access to job postings, candidates, and assignments

### Business Department Roles
- **BDM Director**: Full company management, read job postings, view department metrics
- **Sr BDM Director**: Create, read, update companies; read job postings
- **BDM**: Create, read, update companies; read job postings

### Recruitment Department Roles
- **Recruitment Director**: Full candidate management, read job postings, manage assignments, view department metrics
- **Recruitment Manager**: Create, read, update candidates and assignments
- **Recruitment Lead**: Create, read, update candidates; create and read assignments
- **Recruiter**: Create, read, update candidates; read assignments

### Finance Department Roles
- **Finance Director**: View department metrics, read companies and assignments
- **Finance Manager**: Read companies and assignments
- **Finance Lead**: Read companies and assignments
- **Finance Analyst**: Read companies and assignments
